THE PROBLEM

Moving Data Creates Friction

The insurance carriers that can find signal from noise in external data the fastest will be the winners in the age of AI. But building these data pipelines requires rapid testing, and that requires moving data. Infosec, legal, and compliance teams play a critical role in protecting sensitive data, which can sometimes introduce processes and safeguards that significantly slow down implementation—but are essential to managing risk responsibly. But what if there was a sandbox to experiment quickly and safely with new data and models, without revealing even a single row of data to your partners?
THE SOLUTION

Collaborate Without Moving Data

integrate.ai’s federated data science platform enables insurance carriers to rapidly run tests that reveal the value of external data or models to their business, without exposing any raw data. This technology is secure and private by design, enforcing infosec and legal policies and controls with mathematically-guaranteed privacy protection.
